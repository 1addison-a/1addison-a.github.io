Adoption Process

Follow these simple steps to welcome a new pet into your family

Step 1: Browse Available Pets

Visit our Available Pets page to explore all the wonderful animals looking for homes. Use our filters to find pets that match your preferences for type, age, and breed.

Step 2: Submit an Application

Once you've found a pet you're interested in, fill out our adoption application form. This helps us understand your living situation, experience with pets, and what you're looking for in a companion.

Step 3: Meet and Greet

After your application is reviewed and approved, we'll schedule a meet-and-greet with your potential new pet. This gives you time to interact and ensure it's a good match for both you and the animal.

Step 4: Home Visit

Our team will conduct a brief home visit to ensure your living space is safe and suitable for your new pet. This is also a great opportunity to ask any questions you may have.

Step 5: Finalize Adoption

Complete the adoption paperwork and pay the adoption fee. All pets come vaccinated, spayed/neutered, and microchipped. We'll provide you with medical records and care instructions.

Step 6: Welcome Home!

Take your new family member home! We provide ongoing support and resources to help you and your pet adjust to life together. Don't hesitate to reach out if you need assistance.

Required Documents

Please have the following documents ready:

  • Valid photo ID
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or lease agreement)
  • Veterinary references (if you've had pets before)
  • Landlord approval (if renting)

Adoption Fees

Our adoption fees help cover the cost of veterinary care, food, and shelter for our animals:

  • Dogs: $200
  • Cats: $150
  • Senior pets (8+ years): $100

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about pet adoption

How long does the adoption process take? +
The adoption process typically takes 1-2 weeks from application submission to taking your pet home. This timeframe allows us to properly review applications, conduct meet-and-greets, and complete home visits to ensure the best match.
Can I adopt if I rent my home? +
Yes! Many renters successfully adopt pets. You'll need to provide written permission from your landlord confirming that pets are allowed. We recommend checking your lease agreement for any breed or size restrictions before applying.
Are your pets vaccinated and spayed/neutered? +
Yes! All of our pets are up-to-date on vaccinations, spayed or neutered, and microchipped before adoption. We provide complete medical records and information about any ongoing care needs.
What if the adoption doesn't work out? +
We understand that sometimes things don't work out as planned. If you need to return a pet within the first 30 days, we'll work with you to find a solution. Our goal is always the well-being of both the pet and the adopter.
Do you offer trial periods? +
We offer a 2-week trial period for all adoptions. This gives you and your new pet time to adjust and ensures the placement is a good fit. During this time, we're available to answer questions and provide support.
Can I meet multiple pets before deciding? +
Absolutely! We encourage you to meet several pets to find the right match. You can schedule visits with multiple animals during the same visit or come back for additional meet-and-greets.
Do you have pets suitable for families with children? +
Yes! Many of our pets are great with children. We carefully evaluate each animal's temperament and can recommend pets that would be perfect for families. During the adoption process, we consider your family composition to ensure a good match.
What kind of support do you provide after adoption? +
We provide lifetime support for all our adopters! This includes access to training resources, behavioral advice, and ongoing guidance. You can always reach out with questions or concerns, and we're here to help you and your pet thrive together.

Have more questions? We're here to help!
